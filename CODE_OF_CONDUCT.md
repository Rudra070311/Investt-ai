# Code of Conduct

Investt is committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone.

This applies to:
- Contributors
- Reviewers
- Maintainers
- Community members

---

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Accept feedback gracefully
- Help others learn
- Assume good intent
- Focus on ideas, not individuals

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination of any kind
- Personal attacks
- Hate speech or harmful language
- Encouraging unsafe financial behavior
- Sharing private or sensitive information

---

## Enforcement

Maintainers have the right to:
- Remove comments
- Close issues or PRs
- Temporarily or permanently ban contributors

We don’t enjoy doing this — but safety comes first.

---

## Reporting Issues

If you see behavior that violates this code:
- Report it privately to the maintainers
- Do not escalate publicly

---

## Final Thought

We’re all learning.  
Let’s build something meaningful — together.

— Investt Team
